Work Term: Fall 2021

IT Support at the University of Guelph

October 21, 2021 to December 22, 2021

Employer Information

My employer was Physical Resources of the University of Guelph. I assisted with the information technology (IT) department of Physical Resources. Physical Resources builds, operates, and maintains physical assets of the University. They provide support services to the entire Campus.

Goals

My goal was to improve my skills with instruction giving and problem-solving. I improved on these skills when I was required to help clients with any technical issues and create How-To documents for users. When problems arise from clients, I gather as much information as possible about them. Then I would give them solutions from past situations or research if it is a new one. These situations aided the completion of my goals, but improvements are possible.

I also had a goal to learn and use ASP.NET. I learned how to use ASP.NET and created a website to play around with the features that it supplies. I could not apply the knowledge I learned in this work term. I hope this knowledge will help me in the future.

Job Description

Some basic tasks that I've done in my work term are:

  • - On-Call Technology Assistance
  • - Software and Hardware Upgrades/Updates
  • - Workstation Installations
  • - Asset Management

A significant project that occurred during my work term was server migrations. This task entailed hardware upgrading, profile transferring, updating DNS, and joining the new domain.

I was assigned the task of designing and creating 3D prints. This task allowed me to learn how to use a modeling program called Fusion 360 to create models. I then used Simiplify3D to convert my models into files read by a 3D printer to print out the model. It helped improve my problem-solving skills as there are many variables to 3D printing to create successful prints.

Conclusions

In all, this was an enjoyable work term where I learned a lot, met great people, and I progressed further with my goals. Thank you for reading my reflections upon this work term.
